About

There was a time when Gods walked the earth alongside man. Wars were fought with swords and with courage, heroes did what needed to be done, and damsels were the ones who caused all the distress.

Sacrifices were but prayers to the gods, and there was no gesture nobler than offering your life for your beliefs.

Hospitality meant offering your kingdom to a beggar, and revenge meant slaughtering entire bloodlines of your nemesis.

We would never dream of doing such things, going through the trials and tribulations that have been mythicized - not in the safe, secure world we live in.

But a man who has been through bitter experiences and travelled far, enjoys even his sufferings after a time.

So, join us on a journey of epic proportions, from the shores of Ithaca to the temples of Athens, from the jungles of Ayodhya to the libraries of Taxila, from the branches of Yggdrasil and through the gates of Valhalla!

Some things you will think of yourself. Some things we will put into your mind. Some songs will elate the spirit. Some dances will energize the body. All days will reinvent the mind.

Let us not then die ingloriously and without a struggle, but let us first do great things that shall be told – a tale that will live among Gods and Men forever.
